It is 11:47 PM on Friday. You are a board member of Meridian Bank. An internal audit has just surfaced a critical AI fairness flaw - hours before a $4M marketing campaign launches Monday morning.

📋 Read the Full Situation Briefing

The Product: PrismScore is Meridian's AI-powered credit scoring engine, cutting loan decisions from 5 days to under 2 hours. It is core to the "Better Decisions. Faster." strategic pillar - already announced to shareholders.

The Problem: A late Friday fairness audit detected disparate impact against applicants aged 55+ and those in regional postcodes. Root cause: proxy variables in historical repayment data - AI inheriting systemic bias.

The Stakes: 1.2 million customers. Regulatory exposure. ESG commitments. Shareholder trust. Employee morale. And a Monday launch in 60 hours.

⚠ Game Over Conditions - Know Before You Vote

Revenue hits $0 - The company enters voluntary administration. Game over immediately.
Shareholder Satisfaction hits 0 - The board is voted out at an emergency general meeting.
Employee Morale hits 0 - The entire workforce goes on indefinite strike. Operations cease.
